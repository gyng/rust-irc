Required Methods

fn to_irc_lower(&self) -> T

Makes a copy of the string in IRC ASCII lower case: ASCII letters 'A' to 'Z' are mapped to 'a' to 'z', and "[]\~" are mapped to "{}|^" respectively, but all other characters are unchanged.

fn eq_ignore_irc_case(&self, other: &Self) -> bool

Check that two strings are an ASCII case-insensitive match. Same as to_irc_lower(a) == to_irc_lower(b), but without allocating and copying temporary strings.
